Privacy Policy Statement

This Privacy Policy is designed to clearly explain our rules regarding the collection, storage, and use of information when you visit, download, or upgrade our products, so as to protect your legitimate rights and privacy security.

I. Scope of Information Collection

  • (A) Personal Information
    We do not collect any information that can be used to identify your personal identity, namely "Personal Information", including but not limited to your name, email address, physical address, calendar entries, contact information, files, photos, etc.

    We collect anonymous information related to your use of our applications, namely "Non-Personal Information", specifically covering the type of mobile device you use, mobile operating system, application usage patterns, as well as iOS version, installation data, usage frequency, country of residence, device information, and channels.
    The purpose of collecting this information is to better understand user behavior, address issues in products and services, and optimize our products and services.
    It should be noted that if non-personal information is combined with personal information, the combined information will be treated as personal information under this Privacy Policy.

II. Principles for Information Use and Disclosure

We will only use the personal information you submit for the following purposes: to help us develop, share, and improve our products and services, and to provide you with higher-quality services.
We will only disclose your personal information in the following circumstances:

  • With your explicit authorization;
  • As required by laws and regulations.
Meanwhile, we commit that no specific data related to end-users will be shared or sold to third parties. We have no intention of storing users' personal information or data, nor do we track your browsing history.

III. Information Security Safeguards

We attach great importance to the confidentiality of your information. Since we do not collect personal information, our security measures primarily focus on non-personal information. We employ administrative, physical, and electronic measures to prevent unauthorized access and use, ensuring the security of your information.

IV. Users' Privacy Rights

Except as explicitly stated in the application, we do not store or retain any personal information that can be used to identify you (the end-user). Your privacy rights are always a key focus for us, and we are committed to providing you with a secure and reliable service environment.
